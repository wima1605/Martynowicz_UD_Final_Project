Introduction

Welcome to the Universal Design website. Our mission is to make the web accessible to everyone, regardless of ability or disability. Learn more about why universal design is essential, what it entails, and how you can apply these principles to your projects.

Benefits of Universal Design

Universal design benefits everyone by creating a more inclusive environment. It allows people of all abilities to access information, products, and services without barriers. Implementing universal design principles not only helps those with disabilities but also improves the overall user experience for everyone.

Examples of Universal Design

Here are some examples of how universal design can make a difference:

  • Websites with clear navigation and accessible content for screen readers.
  • Color schemes that meet contrast requirements for users with visual impairments.
  • Mobile-first responsive layouts that work seamlessly on all devices.

Get Involved

If you are interested in making the web more accessible, start by learning about universal design principles and applying them to your own projects. Together, we can create a more inclusive web for everyone.
